About Me

Crafting 3D Worlds

I'm Nicholas Smyth, a driven 3D artist based in Tampa/Orlando, Florida, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Digital Media at the University of Central Florida with minors in Computer Science and Communications. Anticipated graduation: May 2026.

With 3+ years of experience in 3D modeling, I've developed a strong foundation in creating game-ready assets using Maya as my primary modeling tool, complemented by advanced texturing workflows in Substance Painter and real-time integration with Unreal Engine.

My journey includes reverse-engineering complex sci-fi devices, crafting fantasy-themed props, and leading teams to victory at UCF's Game Jam 2024. I'm passionate about pushing the boundaries of digital art while maintaining clean topology and game-ready standards.

Ongoing Project

Wayne Densch Center

Digital sculpt project - Architectural Precision Modeling

Project Technical Goals

My primary objective with this project is to master Architectural Precision Modeling. I am learning the technical feats of translating 2D site blueprints into a 1:1 scale 3D environment, ensuring that every structural element matches real-world measurements within a millimeter of tolerance.

This involves mastering Unit Scale synchronization between modeling software and game engines to ensure the model functions as a reliable Digital Twin for construction logistics and site safety planning.
